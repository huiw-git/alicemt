# Job Properties - New Job (Steps Page)
Use this page to view and organize job steps for a [!INCLUDE[msCoName](../content/includes/msCoName_md.md)] [!INCLUDE[ssNoVersion](../content/includes/ssNoVersion_md.md)] Agent job.  
  
## Options  
**Job step list**  
Lists the job steps for this job.  
  
**Move step**  
Moves the job a step up or down in the list.  
  
**Start step**  
Select the step that [!INCLUDE[ssNoVersion](../content/includes/ssNoVersion_md.md)] Agent starts with when the job begins.  
  
**New**  
Create a new job step below the selected job step.  
  
**Insert**  
Create a new job step above the selected job step.  
  
**Edit**  
Change the selected job step.  
  
**Delete**  
Delete the selected job step. When job steps are deleted their output log is automatically deleted.
