# getCharacterStream Method (SQLServerClob)
  Retrieves the **Clob** value designated by the java.sql.Clob object as a java.io.Reader object.  
  
## Overload List  
  
|Name|Description|  
|----------|-----------------|  
|[getCharacterStream Method &#40;&#41;](../content/getCharacterStream-Method---.md)|Returns the Clob data as a java.io.Reader object or as a stream of characters.|  
|[getCharacterStream Method &#40;long, long&#41;](../content/getCharacterStream-Method--long--long-.md)|Returns the Clob data as a java.io.Reader object or as a stream of characters with the specified position and length.|
